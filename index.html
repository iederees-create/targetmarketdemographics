Subscription Plans Overview

This page provides a detailed breakdown of our subscription plans, target markets, and user journeys.

Flowchart: Target Market and Subscription Plan Flow

The flowchart above illustrates how different target markets align with our subscription plans:

  • Basic Plan (R100/month): Targets high school students, pre-law undergraduates, and career changers.
  • Standard Plan (R150/month): Targets law school students, recent graduates, and junior associates.
  • Premium Plan (R300/month): Targets mid-level associates, senior associates, partners, judges, legal academics, and legal entrepreneurs.

Storyboard: User Journey for Each Plan

The storyboard above shows the user journey for each subscription plan:

  • Basic Plan: From exploring legal careers to preparing for law school.
  • Standard Plan: From excelling in law school to securing a job.
  • Premium Plan: From advancing careers to growing practices and influencing the legal profession.

Detailed Plan Breakdown

Basic Plan (R100/month)

  • Target Market: Law matriculants, pre-law undergraduates, career changers.
  • Key Needs: Affordable guidance, mentorship, foundational resources.
  • Goals: Explore legal careers, prepare for law school, transition into law.
  • Value Proposition: Affordable access to essential resources like guides, webinars, and basic mentorship.

Standard Plan (R150/month)

  • Target Market: Law school students, recent graduates, junior associates.
  • Key Needs: Academic support, bar prep, job search assistance, mentorship.
  • Goals: Excel in law school, pass the bar, secure a job, build a legal career.
  • Value Proposition: Comprehensive support for academic and career success, including tutoring, bar prep, and mentorship.

Premium Plan (R300/month)

  • Target Market: Mid-level associates, senior associates, partners, judges, legal academics, legal entrepreneurs.
  • Key Needs: Leadership training, business development, advanced CLE, thought leadership.
  • Goals: Advance careers, grow practices, influence the legal profession.
  • Value Proposition: High-end resources like advanced leadership training, business development coaching, and exclusive networking events.

Marketing Strategy

Basic Plan (R100/month)

  • Target high schools, colleges, and career fairs.
  • Use social media ads targeting students and career changers.
  • Offer free trials or discounted first-month subscriptions.

Standard Plan (R150/month)

  • Partner with law schools and bar associations.
  • Use email marketing to target law students and recent graduates.
  • Highlight success stories and testimonials from users who passed the bar or secured jobs.

Premium Plan (R300/month)

  • Network at legal conferences and events.
  • Use LinkedIn ads to target mid-to-senior-level professionals.
  • Offer exclusive webinars, workshops, and networking events for premium members.
